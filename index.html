Keeping a Compelling Scoreboard KTAF Talent · 26–27
Week of —
Purpose

This is how we keep score.

Our talent team commitments tell us that being a strategic team requires us to focus on the topline, know the score, and act on data. 4DX tells us that in order to do those things, we must keep a Compelling Scoreboard.

A Compelling Scoreboard tells us clearly what it looks like to win — those are our Topline Goals. And it tells us, at any given moment, whether we are winning.

4DX also tells us that an important part of keeping a Compelling Scoreboard is that the players keep score for themselves. Each of us owns our numbers, enters them weekly, and we use that information to inform how we move.

Step 1 — Before Monday
📥

Enter your data

Open the Google Sheet from the Data Entry tab and enter your numbers in the 1 | 26-27 Data tab. Find your rows (sorted by owner) and enter this week's values in the current week's column. Leave blank if no change.

💡 Please have data entered by TBD each week so the board is ready at meeting start.
Step 2 — At the Meeting
📊

Review together

Once all data is in, Laura clicks Sync from Sheet then Generate Headlines & Hot Spots on the Summary & Analysis tab. The board automatically surfaces anything above goal, below goal, or worse than last year. The team reviews and updates Topline Goal statuses inline.

💡 Only Laura hits Generate — after all data is synced from the Sheet.
